Dumbwaiter

A medieval-style lift for moving between vertically-separated floors. Step onto a station, pull the lever, and ride a brief, atmospheric transition — chain rattle, pulley squeal, wood groan, and a fade — to its paired station above or below. Perfect for cliff-side bases, multi-floor towers, mineshafts, and deep cellars.

How it works

Place a Dumbwaiter Station at each end of your shaft, link them with a Rope Link, and you have a working lift. Activating one station whisks you to the other through a short immersive sequence whose length scales with the distance traveled.

Linking two stations

  1. Place one station at the bottom of your shaft, one at the top.
  2. Right-click the first station with the Rope Link → "Dumbwaiter anchor set."
  3. Right-click the second station with the Rope Link → "Dumbwaiter linked! Distance: N blocks."

Both ends now remember each other, and the link item is consumed.

Limits & behavior

  • Max vertical distance: 192 blocks
  • Max horizontal offset: 4 blocks (allows slightly angled shafts)
  • Transition time: ~1–3 seconds, scaling with trip distance
  • Cooldown after arrival: ~0.5–2 seconds, scaling with trip distance
  • The destination must be loaded and unobstructed — the lift will not drop you into a wall.
  • One rider at a time per station (prevents double-activation).

Configurable

A dumbwaiter.json config file (auto-generated in ModConfig) lets you tune max distance, horizontal offset, transition and cooldown timing, sound volume, and an optional toggle for the screen-fade effect.

Compatibility

  • Built for Vintage Story 1.22+
  • Universal (required on client and server); safe for multiplayer.
